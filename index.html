About

LaunchPath delivers personalized 1:1 coaching to transform students and early-career professionals into competitive software engineering candidates. Through targeted guidance, we help you:

  • Define your unique career path in tech
  • Craft standout applications that get noticed
  • Develop the practical skills employers actually value

Services

Career Planning

We identify your ideal SWE specialization, select the most marketable tech stack for your goals, and create a structured roadmap that fits perfectly with your academic or personal schedule.

Internship & Werkstudent Guidance

Navigate the German tech landscape with confidence. Get help finding hidden opportunities, understanding university requirements, and creating applications that showcase your potential—even for companies not actively hiring.

CV, LinkedIn & Cover Letter Coaching

Transform your professional documents into powerful marketing tools. We'll optimize your CV, LinkedIn profile, and application letters specifically for software engineering roles in the German and EU markets.

Interview Preparation

Master both technical and behavioral interviews through realistic mock sessions. Receive detailed feedback and insider knowledge about local hiring practices to stand out from other candidates.

Every session is customized to address your unique needs and career objectives.
